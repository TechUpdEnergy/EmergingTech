Ultra-Fast DC Fault Protection Technology

Challenge Addressed

Traditional mechanical HVDC breakers are too slow or complex for reliable protection of Multi-Terminal HVDC (MTDC) systems. Lack of fast and effective DC breakers has been a major impediment to developing meshed DC grids, which are needed for large-scale renewable integration and enhanced grid resilience.

How It Works

Solid-State HVDC Circuit Breakers use power semiconductor devices (instead of mechanical contacts and arc-quenching media) to interrupt high-voltage DC fault currents.
