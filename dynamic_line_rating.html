Real-Time Transmission Line Capacity Optimization

Problem Being Addressed

Traditional static line ratings are conservative, based on worst-case weather assumptions, leading to lines often operating below their true thermal capacity. Bottlenecks in the transmission system limit the dispatch of low-cost renewable energy and can increase operational costs.

How Dynamic Line Rating Works

Dynamic Line Rating (DLR) systems determine the real-time thermal capacity (ampacity) of overhead transmission lines by considering actual local weather conditions and physical line parameters.
