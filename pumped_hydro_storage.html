Charging (Pumping Mode)

During periods of low electricity demand or high renewable energy supply, water is pumped from the lower reservoir to the upper reservoir using electricity from the grid. This converts electrical energy into gravitational potential energy.

‚¨áÔ∏è

Discharging (Generating Mode)

During periods of high electricity demand, water is released from the upper reservoir and flows down through the turbines via penstocks. The turbines spin the generators, converting potential energy back into electricity.
